What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay your loan. It includes both principal amount and interest.

EMI Formula

EMI = P × R × (1+R)^N / ((1+R)^N - 1)

  • P = Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Number of Months
