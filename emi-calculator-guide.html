What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) वह fixed amount होता है जो आपको हर महीने loan repay करने के लिए देना होता है।

EMI Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1]

  • P = Loan amount
  • R = Monthly interest rate
  • N = Loan tenure (months)
